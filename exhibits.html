The Art of Human Rights. An exhibit of painting and drawings on human rights related topics. In preparation.

 

 

PRELUDE TO THE HOLOCAUST A WWW exhibition - remodeled in August 1998 - picturing patterns of hatred in pre-Holocaust Germany and in East Europe. Prelude to the Holocaust displays pictures and documents on the discrimination and attacks Jews faced in Germany right before and soon after the Nazi came to power. The exhibition points out several similarities shared by anti-Semitism and present-day hatred against homosexuals and transgender persons. An accompanying booklet containing all the photos in the exhibit is now available.
