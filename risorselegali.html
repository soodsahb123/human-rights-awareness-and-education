Risorse legali/Legal Resources


The association is in contact with a law firm in Padua for the assistance necessary for the procedure of adaptation of sexual characteristics according to the Italian law No. 164/1982. The law firm has experience in supporting transsexual women, and can operate throughout the national territory (although preferably in Veneto and neighboring regions).
